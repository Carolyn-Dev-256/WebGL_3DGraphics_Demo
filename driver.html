Create your own scene!

Instructions

	Draw a curve by left clicking on the left canvas, and then right click to finalize it. Once you've finalized your curve, 
choose an amount of rotations and whether or not to draw end caps, and then generate your mesh! Once generated, you can
download the coordinate and polygon files of the mesh.
	Once your SOR is generated, several instances of the object will appear on the screen that you can freely manipulate. 
Hold left click to select and drag the option around, after which the object can be rotated with rightmouse and scaled by 
holding middle mouse (drag up/down to scale length and left/right for radius)
	Additionally, the user can toggle both a point and directional lightsource, move the x-y value of point light, toggle ambient 
and specular lighting effects, change the shininess of the shape, and choose between flat, Gouraud, and Phong shading.
After achieving a desired rendering, the user can rotate, zoom, and pan the camera around the scene.
